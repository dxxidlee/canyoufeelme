What makes you happy?

When I talk to my mom about my art, clothing, love life, and how much money I will make in the future.

When I start to sketch right after I get an inspiration for it.

When I start my day off by making a cup of coffee for myself.

What makes you sad?

When I listen to sad songs because I think about my family, sometimes even ending up crying as I'm thinking about how hard my mom worked for me and my brother and thinking about that she could even die tomorrow.

When I feel uneasy or anxious about my future.

When I think about money, it makes me sad for some reason.

What makes you angry?

When I see "bad" art, but some people don't believe in it, making me more upset as I try to persuade them.

When I sometimes also end up making "bad" art; I get really upset.

When I encounter a narcissistic person.
