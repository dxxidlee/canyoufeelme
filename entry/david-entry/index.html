What makes you happy?

When I put effort into planning and it goes all right. There is actually nothing better than that feeling.

When I bike fast in a decently windy weather, the more reckless I bike and the faster I get from point A to point B, the better it is.

When I spend time with people I love, whatever it is and whoever it is: family, friends, anyone.

What makes you sad?

When I realize that my parents won't be with me forever, I hate that feeling.

When I go on a streak of sad videos on social media, especially about parents.

When I look at the mirror too long, I start tweaking.

What makes you angry?

When I try everything in my ability and it still goes wrong, the opposite of what makes me genuinely happy.

When I rush to get somewhere and end up sweating a river, I end up just sweating for 30 minutes after.

When I see, hear, smell, or feel something specific and can't unsee, unhear, unsmell, unfeel it.
